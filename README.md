📊 Business Insight Summary

1. Revenue & Basic Sales KPIs

Total Orders: Count of all orders in the database.

Total Revenue: Sum of all pizzas sold (quantity × price).

Avg Pizzas per Order: Helps understand order size and customer spend patterns.

Business Value:
• Identifies baseline performance of the business.
• Assists in forecasting revenue and planning staffing/inventory.

2. Order Trends Over Time

Hourly distribution: Reveals peak hours (e.g., lunch vs. dinner).

Daily & Weekly patterns: Shows high traffic days (e.g., weekends).

Cumulative revenue trends: Highlights seasonality or growth over time.

Business Value:
• Optimize labor scheduling based on demand patterns.
• Target promotions during slow periods.

3. Product Performance

Top pizza types by revenue and quantity: Identifies best-selling and least-selling items.

Category vs. Size performance: Determines which segments contribute most to revenue.

Business Value:
• Menu optimization — focus marketing on high performers.
• Consider discounting or removing low performers.
• Inform pricing strategies.

4. Advanced Insights

Revenue percentage contributions: Understand the relative impact of each product.

Top performers per category: Meets nuanced business needs (e.g., “top 3 chicken pizzas by revenue per category”).

Comparative metrics across categories and times.

Business Value:
• Supports targeted promotions (e.g., lunch combos, category campaigns).
• Enables high-value inventory planning and supply chain decisions.
